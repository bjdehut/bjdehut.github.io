CART! - My Friend's Death
I filmed this concert back in 2003 with the intentions of creating a music video for the band. Over the last 16 years I lost the VHS tape, found it, broke it, repaired it, converted it, ripped it, edited it, and finally today I am posting it.



Easter Egg - The Heist
Portland DJ Easter Egg is renowned for his ability to layer dozens of tracks together to make some amazing dance sets. For his project The Heist he tasked me with blending some videos to match his mix. The result is the copyright infringement smorgasbord found above.



Enclosed Rhythm Exposed

Panther Transformation Sequence
I was listening to a lot of Hypnotize Minds (specifically La’ Chat) around the time I found out I could download videos from YouTube. I always wanted to make music videos but I didn’t really know any musicians and I couldn’t really afford any video equipment. I spent an entire night crafting my dream music video for La’ Chat’s U Don’t Want None from a few other music videos.
