Countin' My Spokes Episode 002: Kings Of NY


Some of the greatest rappers hail from the birthplace of hip hop. But, tonight we hail the true kings of NY, selecting one from each borough: The King of The Bronx: KRS-One, The King of Manhattan: Cam'Ron, The King of Brooklyn: Notorious B.I.G., The King of Staten Island: Ghostface Killah, and The King of Queens: Nas.
