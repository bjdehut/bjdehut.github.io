Cheers Bandini! Cheers Chinaski!
In my youth, I fled Portland to the furthest place I could afford – Los Angeles. This roman-a-clef style novel is dedicated to the works of John Fante (pseudonym Arturo Bandini) and Charles Bukowski (pseudonym Henry Chinaski). Through their stories I found a connection in this world, reading these men having the same ill thoughts I was having in the same city I was living made me feel less alone.
